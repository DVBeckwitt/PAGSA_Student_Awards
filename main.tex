and other honors for Physics \& Astronomy graduate students at Mizzou, 
spanning approximately 2019 through 2024 featured in the newsletters.

inconsistent, please let us know!}

Some awards spanned multiple years or appear in multiple newsletters.  
Additional minor distinctions (e.g.\ small conference travel grants not 
specifically named) may not appear here unless they were featured prominently.

\section*{Graduate Student Awards and Recognitions (2019--2025)}

\noindent
\emph{This document compiles awards, scholarships, fellowships, recognitions,
and other honors for Physics \& Astronomy graduate students at Mizzou,
spanning approximately 2019 through the 2025 department newsletter.
If you notice any entry missing or inconsistent, please let me know.}

\medskip
\noindent
\textbf{Contributor:} David Beckwitt

\medskip
\textbf{Remarks:}
Some awards spanned multiple years or appear in multiple newsletters.
Additional minor distinctions (e.g.\ small conference travel grants not
specifically named) may not appear here unless they were featured prominently.
The maintained newsletter provenance notes live in the student source index.
